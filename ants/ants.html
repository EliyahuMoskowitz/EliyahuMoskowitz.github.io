The Rules of the Game

You can create any amount and color of ants and food. When two ants of different colors meet, they FIGHT! The winner takes all the strength of the loser and gets even stronger for the next fight. An ant will die if it does not eat for a certain period of time; when they catch food they detect which home is closest and run straight towards the home to deposit its produce.
